There are many databases available MySQL, Oracle, SQL Server, etc. You should choose your server based on your database requirement.

How Do I Create Email Accounts in cPanel?

To create an email account:
Log into cPanel.
In the Email section, click Email Accounts.
In the Email field, type the name you want and select the domain name from the drop-down menu below.
In the Password fields, type the desired password twice.
Make a selection for Mailbox Quota.
Click Create Account
